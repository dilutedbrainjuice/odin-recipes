Mapo Tofu

Description

A cup of latte is a comforting and velvety coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. This popular beverage features a perfect balance of bold espresso flavor and the creamy texture of frothed milk. It's often topped with a thin layer of microfoam, creating a delightful coffee experience.

Ingredients

  • Espresso beans
  • Fresh milk

Steps

  1. Brew a shot of espresso using your preferred method.
  2. Heat fresh milk in a steam wand or on the stovetop until it's warm and frothy.
  3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso shot in a cup.
  4. Create a thin layer of microfoam on top by holding back the froth with a spoon while pouring.
  5. Optional: Dust the top with a sprinkle of cocoa or cinnamon for added flavor.
  6. Enjoy your delicious cup of latte!
